About HapHappens

The Covid-19 pandemic has been a strain on our mental health. It's tough enough to be socially isolated, but when all you hear and see is bad news, it's even more difficult to visualize a positive hopeful path forward.

Look around... there's goodness out there.

Good news may be hard to find, but everyday, great people do great things.

The idea for HapHappens started out of frustration of being constantly pestered with bad news.

I wanted to create a space for kids to see the good in people and in our world. Kindness, empathy, creativity, community, and a lot fo bad humor. I want the kindness of others to inspire us so that we can all be kinder to each other. I want kids to have hope for our future.
I hope HapHappens can remind us all that Happiness Happens all around us.
